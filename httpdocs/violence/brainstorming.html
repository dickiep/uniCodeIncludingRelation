Brainstorming

To get started, form into a group of three or four and discuss what you know, or you think you know, about video games and violence. Your initial discussion should be based around the following points:

  • examples of violent games you know/play
  • the type of violence, i.e. fighting, shooting, driving etc...
  • what you feel / people you know say they feel when playing
  • any cases of violence that have been related to video games
  • your initial thoughts about the influence of violent games on behaviour
